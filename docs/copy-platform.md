# SaveWatt Copy Platform

## Messaging Spine

The page should follow:

- `AIDA` at the page level
- `PAS` in the problem block
- strong `risk reversal` throughout

## Primary Differentiators

- `Bureau d'etudes independant`
- `Nous ne vendons pas d'energie`
- `Zero charge pour vos equipes`
- `Audit gratuit`
- `Remuneration uniquement au succes`
- `Recuperation + optimisation + suivi durable`
- `Vous pouvez commencer avec une facture recente`

## Hero Variants

### Option 1

- Headline: `Vous payez peut-etre votre energie trop cher.`
- Subheadline: `SaveWatt audite jusqu'a 5 ans de factures, recupere les trop-percus et optimise durablement vos contrats, sans avance et uniquement au resultat.`
- CTA: `Demander mon audit gratuit`

### Option 2

- Headline: `Recuperez ce que vos factures d'energie vous ont coute en trop.`
- Subheadline: `Nous analysons electricite et gaz, detectons les anomalies, menons les reclamations et securisons vos economies futures.`
- CTA: `Recevoir mon pre-diagnostic`

### Option 3

- Headline: `Chaque ligne de facture peut cacher un gisement d'economies.`
- Subheadline: `SaveWatt identifie les erreurs, recupere les montants indus et recalibre vos contrats pour que les ecarts ne reviennent pas.`
- CTA: `Envoyer une facture`

## Section-By-Section Copy Architecture

| Section | Message goal | Copy direction | Proof | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hero | Explain the offer instantly | Recuperation, optimisation durable, zero upfront risk | `0 EUR`, `5 ans`, recovery potential | `Demander mon audit gratuit` |
| Reassurance strip | Remove suspicion early | Honoraires au resultat, independance, zero internal load | business model facts | `Voir comment ca marche` |
| Le constat | Surface silent losses | Errors in billing, power, taxes, contracts | anomaly examples, 5-year prescription | `Verifier si vous etes concerne` |
| Notre solution | Show full service chain | Audit, recovery, optimisation, renegotiation, CEE, monitoring | scope and method | `Voir le perimetre de l'audit` |
| Comment ca marche | Lower effort perception | Simple 4-step process | mandate simplicity, handled-for-you workflow | `Recevoir mon diagnostic gratuit` |
| Pour qui | Help self-qualification | Collectivites, multi-sites, industry, tertiary | use cases, threshold logic | `Verifier mon eligibilite` |
| Pourquoi SaveWatt | Build preference | Independence, expertise, confidentiality, measurable reporting | trust, RGPD, operating model | `Parler a un expert` |
| Resultats | Convert promise into credibility | illustrative recovery examples and realistic ranges | numbers, cases, disclaimers | `Estimer mon potentiel` |
| FAQ | Remove last objections | Time, documents, remuneration, confidentiality | direct answers | `Lancer mon audit sans engagement` |
| Final CTA | Close with low friction | recap offer + 3-step start | no-risk framing | `Demander mon audit gratuit` |

## Tone Rules

- premium but plainspoken
- technically credible but never dense
- calm, not alarmist
- anti-jargon at the top of each section
- proof-led, not slogan-led

## Secondary Page Copy Needs

### Contact

- who responds
- expected response time
- which documents help
- what happens after submission
- confidentiality reassurance

### A propos

- why SaveWatt exists
- why it does not sell energy
- why independence matters
- operating method and client defense posture

### FAQ

- eligibility
- required invoices
- timeline
- remuneration model
- what happens if nothing is recovered
- who manages the claims

### Legal Pages

- plain French
- clear explanation of how invoice and contract data is used
- storage, rights, legal basis, and contact method

### Audit Request Flow

- Step 1: lightweight qualification
- Step 2: invoice upload or promise of later upload
- Step 3: expectation-setting confirmation

## Copy Guardrails

- Do not lead with vague “innovation” language
- Do not sound like an energy supplier
- Do not overuse “up to 30%” as the main story
- Do not overpromise before proof exists
- Make the first action feel small and safe
