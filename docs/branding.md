# SaveWatt Branding

## Brand Core

SaveWatt is an independent French `bureau d'etudes` focused on auditing energy invoices, recovering undue charges, and optimizing contracts durably.

It is not:

- an energy supplier
- a generic green-tech startup
- a software-first dashboard company

## Positioning

SaveWatt helps organizations that spend materially on electricity and gas recover money already lost, then prevent future leakage.

## Brand Pillars

### Independence

`Nous ne vendons pas d'energie.`
This must be one of the clearest repeated messages on the site.

### Low-risk engagement

- audit gratuit
- remuneration uniquement au succes
- zero avance

### Technical seriousness

- energy billing expertise
- tax and TURPE literacy
- power and contract optimization
- public-sector and multi-site relevance

### Operational relief

- zero charge for internal teams
- SaveWatt handles claims, coordination, and follow-up

### Durable value

- recovery today
- optimization tomorrow
- monitoring over time

## Voice

- calm
- exact
- disciplined
- premium
- helpful

## Tone

- never flashy
- never eco-preachy
- never startup-hyped
- never legalistic at the top of the page
- always concrete before technical

## Approved Messaging Patterns

- `Bureau d'etudes independant`
- `Nous defendons vos interets face aux fournisseurs`
- `Zero avance, honoraires au resultat`
- `Une facture recente suffit pour demarrer`
- `Recuperation, optimisation, suivi durable`

## Avoid

- vague innovation slogans
- generic sustainability tropes
- excessive claims without context
- overuse of “jusqu'a 30 %” as the main story

## Visual Personality

- technical editorial
- sparse and precise
- French consulting premium
- data-aware but not dashboard-heavy

## Trust Signals To Surface

- Paris address
- legal company identifiers
- contact details
- confidentiality handling
- reporting clarity
- sector understanding

## Audience Priority

1. Collectivites and public structures
2. Multi-site tertiary organizations
3. Industrial and logistics sites
4. Other high-bill organizations
