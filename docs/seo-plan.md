# SaveWatt SEO Plan

## SEO Strategy

SaveWatt should launch as a compact multi-page site, not a single-page brochure.
The goal is to support both conversion intent and future organic expansion.

## Page Themes

| Page | Intent | Theme |
|---|---|---|
| `/` | BOFU category capture | `audit facture energie`, `optimisation facture energie`, `recuperation trop-percu energie`, `bureau d'etudes energie` |
| `/solution` | offer explanation | recovery + optimization + follow-up |
| `/methodologie` | objection handling | `audit gratuit energie`, `remuneration au succes`, `comment ca marche` |
| `/services` | service discovery | audit, recovery, contracts, CEE |
| `/secteurs` | qualification | public and multi-site sector relevance |
| `/secteurs/collectivites` | vertical BOFU | `audit facture energie collectivite`, `optimisation depenses energetiques commune` |
| `/secteurs/entreprises-multi-sites` | vertical BOFU | `optimisation facture energie entreprise`, `audit energie multi-sites` |
| `/resultats` | proof and trust | `economies facture energie`, `exemples recuperation trop-percus` |
| `/pourquoi-savewatt` | trust and entity | independence, reporting, expertise |
| `/faq` | long-tail questions | documents, timing, remuneration, eligibility |
| `/contact` | conversion + entity | audit request and local trust |

## Launch SEO Priorities

1. Crawlable multi-page structure
2. Clean page-specific titles and metas
3. Strong internal linking between service and sector pages
4. Clear entity details in footer and contact page
5. FAQ for objections and long-tail capture
6. Results/proof page for trust

## Structured Data Priorities

- `Organization`
- `LocalBusiness` if the Paris office is a real public-facing location
- `BreadcrumbList`
- `Service` on service pages
- `Article` on future resources
- `Person` on future expert bios if used

## Local And Entity Signals

- consistent name, address, phone, and email
- company identifiers where available
- Paris as the entity home
- national service positioning in copy

## Navigation Recommendations

- expose service and sector clusters in the nav
- keep important pages within 2-3 clicks
- include legal and contact links in the footer
- add an HTML sitemap in a later pass if useful

## Legal And Trust Pages

Launch with:

- `mentions-legales`
- `politique-de-confidentialite`
- `cookies`

If tracking is added, cookie handling and consent messaging must be planned with the build phase.

## Phase 2 Expansion

- deeper service pages
- more sector pages
- problem-led pages around billing errors, TURPE, taxes, power calibration, and renegotiation
- case studies
- resource hub
- data-led benchmark content

## Content Rules

- write for users first, not just search crawlers
- keep claims contextualized
- prefer evidence and specificity over generic SEO filler
- use FAQs for clarity, not fluff
